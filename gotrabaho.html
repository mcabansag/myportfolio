GO TRABAHO

Online job inquiry – This module will help the job seeker to inquire about a job vacancy to the authorized offices.

Job vacancies real time notification – This module will let the applicant know if there is a job vacancy to be posted on the mobile and web application.

Job requirements - This module will manage the requirements needed by the applicants.

Job position - This module will manage the job positions to be hired by the companies.

Company profile - This module will manage the company profile to be posted on mobile and web applications.

Statistics – This module will analyze all the information to provide statistics in the system. The statistic report will include all the job applicants that the City Government of Cabuyao helped to have their jobs, also those workers that have renewed and completed their contract.

Regulatory report generation – This module will manage generating reports that will help the City Cooperative and Livelihood Development Office (CCLDO) to their decision making and improve their services that will help the City of Cabuyao.
